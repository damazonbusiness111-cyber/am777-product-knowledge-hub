How AM777 Pricing Works

The setup fee builds and deploys the system. The monthly system care keeps the system monitored, debugged, maintained, and improved.

One-Time Setup Fee

Covers system design, configuration, testing, and deployment. The setup fee is paid once before the system goes live.

Monthly System Care

Covers system support, debugging, automation checks, workflow monitoring, form/email/CRM fixes, minor updates, 24/7 automated error alerting, and human debugging based on support plan.

How VAs Should Explain AM777

AM777 builds business automation systems that help clients capture leads, organize CRM records, automate follow-ups, reduce manual admin work, and keep workflows monitored through monthly system care.

Do Say

  • Setup builds the system.
  • Monthly care keeps it monitored, debugged, and maintained.
  • AM777 uses automation alerts and human debugging support.
  • Main focus is business systems, onboarding, follow-up, and high-ticket workflows.

Do Not Say

  • 24/7 human team monitoring
  • Guaranteed income
  • Investor-ready company
  • Unlimited support
  • Anything can be built instantly
